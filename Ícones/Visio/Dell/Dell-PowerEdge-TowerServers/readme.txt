Dell Stencils

The suggested stencil directory for Visio 2003, and Visio 2007 is My Documents\My Shapes\Dell

All stencils work best on pages of scale 1:2 to 1:30 Do not use scale of 1:1 (no scale). 


Visio Cafe provides a place for the public to download free Visio stencils from the technology industry.  Our goal is to host the Visio collections of as many vendors as possible and to be the 'go to' site for all Visio stencils, including those that are Microsoft developed (VNE), Vendor self developed, and even individually developed.

If your company would like to host your Visio stencils here for free, please contact us at info@visiocafe.com. 

The Dell stencils are developed by VSD Grafx Inc.  If your company would like to have Visio stencils developed, please contact  info@vsdfx.com.
